Twin Cities Hope and Healing Center LLC

At Twin Cities Hope & Healing Center, we believe that every person deserves support, understanding, and a chance to build a better future. Our mission is simple: to walk alongside adults in our community as they work toward recovery, wellness, and independence.

LEARN MORE

How We Can Help

We provide compassionate mental health and recovery services designed to meet people where they are. Whether you're looking for therapy, coaching, or everyday life support, our team is here to help.

Life & Social Skills Support

We help adults strengthen the everyday skills needed for independence and confidence. This can include building healthy routines, managing stress, improving communication, and feeling more comfortable in social situations. Our goal is to give you tools you can use in real life — at home, work, or in the community.

Certified Peer Support

Sometimes the best support comes from someone who's been through similar struggles. Our Certified Peer Specialists share their own recovery experiences and walk alongside you with understanding and encouragement. They can help with motivation, problem-solving, and showing that healing is possible.

Community Intervention

Life challenges don't always happen in an office. Our team can meet you where you are — in your home or community — to provide guidance, coaching, and support. We focus on helping you stay safe, connected, and on track with your goals.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one therapy gives you a safe, private space to talk through life's challenges. Together with a licensed professional, you'll explore what's holding you back, learn new coping skills, and work toward lasting change at your own pace.

Family Counseling

We help families improve communication, resolve conflict, and support each other in healthy ways. Whether it's parents and children, couples, or extended family, our goal is to strengthen your relationships and help your family heal together.

Group Counseling

In group sessions, you'll connect with others who share similar experiences. Groups provide encouragement, new perspectives, and a sense of community. Together, you'll learn coping skills and practice tools that can support your recovery.

Wellness & Recovery Coaching

Recovery is about more than overcoming challenges — it's about building a full, meaningful life. Our coaches work with you on setting goals, staying motivated, and developing healthy habits. We focus on your strengths and help you take steady steps toward the future you want.

Our Approach

At Twin Cities Hope and Healing, we believe in treating the whole person. Our holistic approach considers your unique background, experiences, and goals to create a personalized treatment plan that works for you.

Our team of licensed professionals combines evidence-based practices with compassionate care to help you navigate life's challenges and develop lasting resilience and well-being. We meet you where you are in your journey.
