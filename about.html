About Us

Learn about our mission, values, and commitment to providing exceptional mental health care.

Our Story

Wellness Center was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide accessible, compassionate mental health care to our community.

What began as a small practice with just three therapists has grown into a comprehensive mental health center serving thousands of individuals throughout Minnesota.

Despite our growth, our core values remain the same: we believe in treating each person with dignity and respect, providing evidence-based care, and meeting individuals where they are in their journey.

Our Mission & Values

These core principles guide everything we do, from how we interact with clients to how we design our treatment programs.

Compassionate Care

We approach each individual with empathy, understanding, and respect, recognizing the courage it takes to seek help.

Evidence-Based Practices

Our treatments are grounded in research and proven approaches, ensuring you receive the most effective care possible.

Holistic Approach

We consider the whole person—mind, body, and spirit—in our approach to mental health and wellness.

Accessibility

We strive to make mental health care accessible to all through flexible scheduling, telehealth options, and insurance acceptance.

Collaboration

We believe in working together—with clients, families, and other providers—to achieve the best possible outcomes.

Continuous Growth

We are committed to ongoing learning and improvement, staying current with the latest advances in mental health care.

Our Approach

At Wellness Center, we believe that effective mental health care must be personalized, comprehensive, and collaborative.

We begin with a thorough assessment to understand your unique needs, strengths, and challenges. From there, we work together to develop a treatment plan that addresses your specific goals.

Our integrated approach combines evidence-based therapies, medication management when appropriate, skill-building, and community support to help you achieve lasting wellness.
