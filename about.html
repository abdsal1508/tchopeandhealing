About Twin Cities Hope & Healing

Building strength, step by step — together with our community

Our Mission & Values

Cultivating hope and mental wellness

At Twin Cities Hope and Healing Center LLC, we are committed to fostering hope and improving mental health in our community. Our organization was founded with a passion for contributing to post-pandemic recovery efforts, addressing the mental health challenges brought to the forefront by COVID-19.

The pandemic highlighted the urgent need for mental health support in our communities. Prolonged isolation, uncertainty, and economic challenges have increased stress and anxiety levels, while many people have been pushed to the edge of hopelessness. We're here to provide that support.

How We Support You

Diagnostic Assessments

Comprehensive diagnostic assessments conducted in the comfort of your own home, creating a safe and familiar environment for evaluation.

Comprehensive Analysis

A written assessment by a mental health professional detailing a person's current life situation, sources of stress, and reasons for referral.

Past Experiences Review

An overview of the person's history, including significant developmental events, strengths, and vulnerabilities that inform treatment planning.

Mental Health Status

An overview of current functioning and symptoms, diagnosis clarification, and identification of mental health services needed.

Rehabilitative Services for Adult Mental Health

Our rehabilitative services utilize a combination of individual in-home services and group sessions to teach management and recovery skills to adults experiencing serious mental illness. We focus on practical tools you can use in daily life.

Mental Health Counseling

Our psychotherapy services involve face-to-face treatment of mental health challenges through psychological and interpersonal methods aimed at achieving measurable goals. We emphasize cultural diversity and provide culturally sensitive services.
