About Us

Your Path to Mental Well-being

OUR VALUED AND REWARDED

Cultivating hope and mental wellness

Twin cities hope and healing center LLC, we are committed to fostering hope and improving mental health. Our organization was founded from my passion for contributing to the post-pandemic recovery efforts addressing the mental health challenges brought to the forefront by COVID-19.

The pandemic has highlighted the urgent need for mental health support in our communities. Prolonged lockdowns and social distancing have increased stress, while business closures and job losses have pushed many to the edge of hopelessness.

How We Support You

Diagnostic Assessments

Diagnostic assessments are conducted in the comfort of your own home.

Analysis

A written assessment by a mental health professional detailing a person's current life situation, sources of stress, and reasons for referral.

Past Experiences

An overview of the person's current mental health issues, including significant developmental events, strengths, and vulnerabilities.

Mental Health Status

An overview of the person's current functioning and symptoms, their diagnosis, and the mental health services they require.

Rehabilitative Services for Adult Mental Health

Rehabilitative Services for Adult Mental Health utilize a combination of individual in-home services and group sessions to teach management and recovery skills to individuals experiencing serious mental illness.

Mental Health Counseling

Psychotherapy services involve face-to-face treatment of mental health illnesses through psychological and interpersonal methods aimed at achieving measurable goals. We emphasize cultural diversity and provide culturally sensitive services.
