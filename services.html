Our Services

Supporting Your Mental Well-being
We offer a range of therapeutic services designed to help you overcome challenges and achieve a healthier, more balanced life.

Basic Living and Social Skills Support

Our team helps you strengthen essential skills for everyday life and independence. This can include improving your ability to manage your household, handle your finances, build healthy routines, and navigate social situations with more confidence. Services can be provided one-on-one or in a group setting.

Community Intervention

We provide direct support by working with you and others in your life, such as your family, employer, or landlord. Our goal is to offer coaching and guidance to help you manage challenges and stay connected within your community.

Medication Education

We offer instruction to help you understand your prescription medication regimen. This service is provided by a qualified physician, pharmacist, or nurse, ensuring you have the knowledge to manage your health effectively.

Certified Peer Specialist Services

Our Certified Peer Specialists offer support from a place of lived experience. They use their own recovery journeys to provide encouragement, show you that healing is possible, and help you find your strengths and resources.

Transition to Community Living Services

We provide specialized support for individuals transitioning from a higher level of care, such as a regional treatment center or hospital, back into the community. We'll help you navigate this transition smoothly and successfully.

Functional Assessments

A Functional Assessment is a detailed evaluation that describes how your mental health symptoms impact your day-to-day life. This assessment helps us understand how your symptoms affect your ability to manage work, relationships, social interactions, and home life. We also consider other factors, such as your personal circumstances and resources, to get a holistic view of your situation
