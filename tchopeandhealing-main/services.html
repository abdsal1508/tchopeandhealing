Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of mental health services designed to support your unique needs and goals.

Individual Therapy

Our one-on-one therapy sessions provide a safe, confidential space to address your specific challenges and work toward your personal goals.

Our therapists are trained in various evidence-based approaches, including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Dialectical Behavior Therapy, and Psychodynamic Therapy.

What to Expect:

  • Initial assessment to understand your needs and goals
  • Collaborative development of a personalized treatment plan
  • Regular sessions focused on building skills and making progress
  • Ongoing evaluation and adjustment of your treatment approach

Group Therapy

Our group therapy sessions provide a supportive community where you can connect with others facing similar challenges, share experiences, and learn from one another.

Groups are facilitated by experienced therapists and focus on specific topics or populations, such as anxiety management, depression support, or trauma recovery.

Current Groups:

  • Anxiety Management (Mondays, 6-7:30 PM)
  • Depression Support (Wednesdays, 5-6:30 PM)
  • Trauma Recovery (Thursdays, 6-7:30 PM)
  • Mindfulness Practice (Tuesdays, 12-1 PM)

Medication Management

Our psychiatric providers offer expert medication management services to help address symptoms of mental health conditions as part of a comprehensive treatment approach.

We believe in a collaborative approach to medication, working closely with you to find the right treatment while minimizing side effects and maximizing benefits.

Our Approach:

  • Thorough evaluation of your symptoms and medical history
  • Education about medication options, benefits, and potential side effects
  • Regular follow-up appointments to monitor progress and adjust as needed
  • Coordination with your therapy team for integrated care

Community Integration

Our community integration services help individuals build meaningful connections and develop the skills needed to thrive in their daily lives.

We provide support with housing, employment, education, and social connections to help you achieve greater independence and well-being.

Services Include:

  • Housing assistance and independent living skills
  • Employment support and vocational training
  • Educational guidance and resources
  • Social skills development and community connection

Specialized Programs

Trauma Recovery Program

A comprehensive approach to healing from trauma using evidence-based therapies and holistic support.

Mindfulness & Stress Reduction

Learn practical techniques to manage stress, reduce anxiety, and cultivate greater awareness and peace.

Family Support Services

Resources and guidance for families supporting loved ones with mental health challenges.
